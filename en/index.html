How it works

Three simple steps to start comparing prices across stores.

1

Install the extension

Add Product Search Button to your browser from the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, or Edge Add-ons.

2

Browse any supported store

Navigate Walmart, Amazon, Mercado Libre, Rappi, Uber Eats, and 20+ other Mexican online stores and delivery services.

3

Click the search button

A search button appears on every product card. Click it to search that product across marketplaces, supermarkets, and deal sites.

Features

Everything you need to search smarter, not harder.

🔍

Auto-detect product cards

Works on 25+ Mexican e-commerce, supermarket, and delivery sites. Detects products automatically as you browse.

🎯

One-click cross-store search

Search the same product on Google, Perplexity, marketplaces, supermarkets, or deal sites with a single click.

⚙️

Customizable selectors

Adjust product card, name, description, and price selectors per site. Adapts quickly when store layouts change.

📋

Priority search groups

Organize search destinations by category — supermarkets, delivery, marketplaces, department stores, and deals.

🛠️

Intuitive options panel

Add new sites, tweak detection, reorder priority stores, and customize the search menu from a simple settings page.

🔒

Privacy first

All settings stored locally. No user accounts, no browsing history collection, no data transmitted to our servers.
