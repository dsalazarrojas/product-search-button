Product Search Button

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

What the extension does

Product Search Button adds a button to product cards on supported online stores. After you choose to use it, the extension helps construct a search query so you can manually search for that product on Google, Perplexity, supermarkets, marketplaces, department stores, and deal sites.

Information processed

The current public version processes only what is needed to provide its features:

  • It stores user settings locally in your browser, such as search-engine configuration, default search engine, menu position, and submenu direction.
  • After you interact with the button, it reads visible product information from supported webpages to construct a search query.

The extension does not create user accounts and does not collect names, email addresses, passwords, payment data, or browsing history. It does not sell personal information.

What the current version does not do

The current public version has no price-history feature, no Google Analytics backend, and no affiliate links or affiliate tracking. Product information is not transmitted to our servers in the current version.

Third-party websites

When you click a search option, the extension opens a third-party website selected by you. Google, Mercado Libre, Temu, AliExpress, and other destinations have their own privacy policies and terms. Review those policies before using the third-party site. We do not control how those sites process information.

Changes to this policy

If the extension’s data practices change, this policy will be updated and the new effective date will be shown here.
